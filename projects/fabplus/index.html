Fabplus - 30 years of experience in machinery

Fabplus was created to offer a better experience when it comes to the windows fabrication industry. With 30 years of experience of making windows we can help you become more efficient, increase production without having to add more employees.

A strong service team is allowing us to fix in less than 24h most problems. This alone makes all the difference when in a production rush with tight delivery deadlines.

We keep most parts in our inventory. This is a godsend when a machine breaks and you have a machine shutdown. We can also fabricate parts or buy equivalents locally. But in the last resort, if for any reason we donâ€™t have the part, it can be courrier to your location in a matter of a few days.

Founder and president Michel Leblond has been working in the fenestration industry since 1995. He was then in charge of uPVC windows production. Having discovered a taste for machinery, 5 years later he takes on the role of maintenance director. Making sure that all machines were running optimally and that the down time was reduced to a minimum was the main goal. In 2000, he starts his own business and has been serving the fenestration industry since then.

It is with this mindset that Michel with the Fabplus team is now helping windows fabrication customers everywhere.

Our team is made of designers, technicians, machinists, managment and sales. The best team to support you for years to come.
